Experience auctions online: An auction house on the Internet - the Auctionata principle

A traditional auction house accessible via the internet; that was the idea behind Auctionata. Based on an international network of experts, Auctionata offers its customers a unique service as well as a whole new distribution model. Thanks to the global reach of the internet, and our offices both in Berlin and New York, we value your fine art and collectibles, and sell them for you via our livestream auctions or in our online shop. Thanks to our patented real-time system, you can track all of our auctions online, and be sure not to miss any our exceptional offers. Due to modern technology, we can offer you lower fees than offline auction houses and our audited and transparent pricing system contributes greatly to the satisfaction of our clients. Our international network of experts, which includes leading experts in their respective fields, value your objects for free, without obligation and professionally. Each item purchased from Auctionata comes with the Auctionata Guarantee, so you can be sure both as a buyer and seller: Auctionata stands for quality.

Buy with an auction house online

Our principal aim is to provide both buyers and sellers with the best all-round service at every auction. Buyers have the choice between selling their items in our livestream auctions or in the Auctionata online shop. In our online shop you will find a wide range of top quality antiques and fine art. Twenty-four hours, seven days a week, you can buy items securely, at reasonable prices, with the Auctionata Guarantee. If you are not satisfied with your purchases, you are welcome to make use of our 14-day money back offer, and we of course accept return shipments. Alternatively, you could treat yourself at one of our livestream auctions. Our auctions offer perfect service, low fees and the chance to buy real rarities at a fair price.

Online auctions – reach the world

Auctionata has offices in Berlin, New York and London, and via the internet we can connect with people all over the globe. As a seller, you have the world at your fingertips. We stream the auctions live on the Internet and are thus able to reach a very large number of potential bidders and interested parties at each auction. Request a valuation to take advantage of our unique, all-round service and have up to five of your items valued by our experts for free and without obligation. We will take care of professional image editing and presenting your items in our catalog. Whether it is shipping insurance or delivery, we will take immediate action. Would you like an advance prior to the auction? This too is not a problem; we are able to pay you up to 20 percent of the estimated price. You also have the option to sell your items in our shop at a fixed price. For all properties available for sale, we offer the Auctionata Guarantee and ensure a quick and easy sale.
